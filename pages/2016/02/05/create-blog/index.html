Tutorials on Storage and Disaster Recovery

Date 五 05 二月 2016 Tags Back-up / Storage / Recovery Lang en en en en en

The tutorial aims at providing undergraduates with guidelines, which are parts of the on-line information security education platform, to explore further in the area of recovery, storage and back-up in the modern operation systems. My work focuses on the recovery, storage and back-up on Linux and a single PC. The tutorials are in 2 main topics(storage and backup) and 3 sub topics(the storage of Linux, the performance testing of storage and back-up with tools from operation systems). To finish tutorials, I have investigated all kinds of materials, references and even some popular blogs. During the process of compiling, I integrated all the materials I had, designed procedures of experiments and eventually organized them in an unified form. The tutorials are easy to follow that almost all undergraduates who have used give me positive feedbacks.
