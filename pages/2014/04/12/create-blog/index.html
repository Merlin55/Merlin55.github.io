The Design and Implementation of Client System of Field-Rescue

Date 六 12 四月 2014 Tags Android / Medical Application Lang en en en

Overview

The mobile medical aid system, which conforms to the historical trend of the paperless and electronic office, enhances the efficiency of medical personnel in large measure. Taking example by existing mobile medical aid system as well as being specific to the application of rescue after disaster, the article has expounded the design principle, service logic, function and implementation of the field-rescue client in detail. As subsystem of the mobile medical aid system based on cloud platform, the field-rescue client has become the platform of interaction of all elements of rescue, making things convenient for rescue and therapy on account of being applied on mobile devices. I have investigated and surveyed in department of medical aid and consulted relative progress of medical aid, which has laid the foundation of the design of service logic. The client which has compiled models on modules has adopted the design concept of object-oriented and divided the system into six parts, including triage, nurse station ,doctor station, transshipment and guardianship, mobile GIS and inspection. When it comes to the application of function, the client invokes models to carry out extension, which make it easier to maintain and extend. Also, the client has adopted the design of the service bus structure based on message-oriented middleware to meet the needs of invoking each other between heterogeneous modules and realize exchange and transmission of message between them. In conclusion, the client specific to the needs of rescue after disaster and synthesizing all possible elements, has taken advantage of the functional framework of android, established the logic framework of the field-rescue client, as well as accomplished functions of every module. After the preliminary test, the result has indicated that the field-rescue client has validity and feasibility



1. The Overall Design

The field-rescue client, based on cloud platform, is a heterogeneous system which is composed of triage, immigration and ward, the nurse station, the doctor station and mobile GIS of staff location.

3. The Structure of the system

The system is designed in a mixture of B/S and C/S style. Here, the module requiring for high quality computation ability, like GIS label calculation and data processing, is implemented by C/S mode, while the module which needs distributed design, like query for disaster information and the management of goods and materials, is implemented by B/S mode.

4. The Work Flow

  1. When rescuers arrive on site, it will indicate the location by GIS.
  2. Do the primary triage to the wounded by the type of the accident and the clinical manifestation.
  3. After step 2, execute the immigration and ward, transfer the wounded to the hospital immediately. During this process, record the vital sign.
  4. Nursers will register, distribute berths and do medical care. All the stuff after the arrival at hospital will be arranged by the nurse station.
  5. After the nurse station dispatches a message to the doctor station, doctors will carry out further medical care to the wounded and send out medial advice to the nurse station.
  6. Nurses will take care of the wounded, following the medical advice.
